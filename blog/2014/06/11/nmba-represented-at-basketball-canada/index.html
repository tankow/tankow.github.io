June 11, 2014

NMBA Represented at Basketball Canada Events

It's remarkable to think about how many athletes and coaches the Northside Minor Basketball Association has sent to Canadian championships.

In its short history, the NMBA has produced a number of players or had players dress in Lions' gear who have played on the national stage.

This year will not be any different.

With the Basketball Canada age-class Under-15 and Under-17 Championship set for Edmonton in late July, many in the province and Fredericton will be following former Lions.

The Under-15 girls' team has former NMBA Under-13 player Brook Billings on its roster. The Under-17 boys' team has former Lions Jared Stafford and Leo Titarenko playing for Team N.B.

Stafford and Titarenko have a familiar sideline voice to hear. NMBA board member and long-time Leo Hayes High Lions' head coach John Hickey is the head helmsman of the provincial squad.

NMBA bantam girls' Division 1 head coach Ryan Chase, who is Hickey's assistant with Leo Hayes, is the head coach of the BNB Under-15 boys' team.

Both Hickey and Chase coached Team N.B. at last year's Canada Summer Games in Sherbrooke, Que. This summer's tournament, open this year for the first time to scouts from the NCAA, are set for July 25 to July 30 at the Saville Community Sports Centre in Edmonton.

Former Lion Taylor Forsythe is a member of the provincial Under-16 girls' team.

The NMBA is also awaiting word on adding potential players to the BNB Under-14 teams, selected following the Bantam Elite Camp in Fredericton in early July.

Former Bantam A Lion Micah Williams is in the boys' selection camp. Present Bantam A Lions Lucy Beaumont, Torrie Janes and Kali Wilson will attend the girls' selection camp.

The Under-14 teams will participate at Summerfest in Saint John at the Under-15 development level.
