Looking to begin the journey? Check out the Developmental program.

Mini Lions Boys and Girls (Grades 4-6)

The Mini boys and girls programs are for those children being introduced to the Basketball New Brunswick development play. All Mini Lions teams play teams from across the province and may travel outside of New Brunswick for exhibition games. They will play at least two or more tournaments and also participate in BNB Championship weekend tournaments.

Bantam Lions Boys and Girls (Grades 7-8)

Bantam boys and girls are the final step for many before going into high school basketball pursuits. It is at the bantam level where the players can develop into potential BNB elite players, but for the majority, it's an opportunity to play and continue developing their skills.
