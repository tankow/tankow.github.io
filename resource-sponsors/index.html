NMBA Sponsors


Animal Essentials

Animal Essentials is located at the Nashwaak Plaza (Superstore) on the Northside of Fredericton.

Bird Stairs

Bird-Stairs has been a longtime and outstanding support of the NMBA. The company is a throwback to service and community involvement. It's with sponsors like Bird-Stairs that the NMBA is able to operate each year.

Boston Pizza

Boston Pizza has joined the NMBA's sponsorship ranks, helping out with our annual bantam girls' tournament.

Dairy Queen

The NMBA is pleased to announce DQ has joined its sponsorship program. The local DQ outlet has long supported grassroots athletics and that tradition continues.

Delta Hotels

Proud supporter of the NMBA.

Philson Ltd

Many thanks to Philson Ltd., located in New Maryland, for their generosity with sponsorship to the NMBA bantam girls' D1 team in preparation for Eastern Canadians.

Pita Pit

The NMBA would like to thank The Pita Pit for its kind association donation. Enjoy a pita break between games or during tournaments in Fredericton!

Taylor Printing

Taylor Printing Group, located at 225 Alison Blvd. in Fredericton, printed a number of tournament programs and banners for various NMBA teams.
