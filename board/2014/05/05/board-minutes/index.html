May 5, 2014

Board Minutes

Present

Brad Janes, Vicky Patterson, Phil Wilson, Scott Williams, Natasha Bozek, Shelley Foreman, John McGrattan

Absent with Notice John Hickey, Shelley Janes

Meeting called to Order at 7:35.

Motion to adopt agenda (no amendments) moved by Vicky, seconded by Phil.

Approval of April, 2012 minutes moved by Phil, seconded by Vicky.

Old Business

None.

Agenda Items**.

Update on Scholarship Applications, Procedure

There were no applications received from boys. Allison Keane, Alison Patterson and Sara Ripley applied for girls.

Motion - Award Individual $500 Scholarships to Three Recipients. Motion made by John McGrattan, seconded by Shelley Foreman. Carried

Under-13 Update

Brad updated board members and confirmed date of Provincials to be May 24-25.

BNB EDP Update

Brad discussed number of kids at tryouts, team sizes and age classes.

Equipment, Inventory, Storage

Our equipment is spread out in too many places to be effectively managed. We discussed looking into renting storage.

Survey Results

Results of our recent survey will be publishes on our website. Overall board was very pleased with number of responses and results. We will do this survey annually to see feedback.

BNB Meeting update (Bantam Pilot League, Potential Age Class Change)

Discussed U11, U12, U13 and U14 age class teams and 6-12 team new Bantam League pilot.Alexander Gibson Memorial School Re-Purposing Project

Several board members toured building and will be exploring the pros / cons and process of the NMBA purchasing and owning a building.

Present Board Returning/Not Returning John McGrattan and Shelly Foreman will not be reoffering. There has been interest expressed by potential replacements to fill empty board and executive positions.

Treasurer's Report

Outgoing treasurer, John McG distributed current bank statements, discussed remaining receivables and payables. Brad thanked John for his service as Treasurer and thanked Shelly Foreman for her contributions.

President's Report

Brad showed us poster templates for NMBA that will be printed and distributed around Fredericton North. Oct 4th will be Nash Celebration Day 10:00 AM- Noon followed by coaches clinic at Leo Hayes. AGM is scheduled for May 29 at 7:00 PM in the Leo Hayes High School library.

Motion to adjourn at 8:56 made by Brad. Carried.
