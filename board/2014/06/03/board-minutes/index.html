June 3, 2014

Board Minutes

Present

Brad Janes, Vicky Patterson, Scott Williams, Phil Wilson, Natasha Bozek, John Hickey, Ryan Chase, Carolyn Legacy, Shelley Janes

Meeting called to Order at 7:32 by Brad.

Approval of Agenda as amended motion by John, seconded by Vicky. Carried

Approval of Last Board Meeting Minutes motioned by Vicky, seconded by Phil. Carried

Old Business

Application and cheque were sent to KidSport for NMBA to sponsor and have booth at August 2014 Family Fun Day.

Agenda Items

Request for BNB Team Player Funding - Motion made by Phil, seconded by Shelley. Carried

Sponsor Taylor Forsythe in the amount of $100 to go towards her BNB Provincial costs. Cheque will be issued payable to BNB.

Information on Storage Unit Locker

Brad received an estimate of $801 per year cost for a heated storage locker. We are not committing to renting storage at this time.

Purchase of New Equipment

Brad and Ryan to conduct inventory during exam week to see what equipment or uniforms need to be purchased going forward.

Coaching Application Deadline

Deadline for coaching applications to be received is June 15.

Steve Nash Registration

Will be available on website as soon as we get gym confirmation dates from the City of Fredericton. Target date is mid July registration for fall session. On a related note, Brad met with a representative from NBA Canada who is promoting a NBA Junior Program for ages 5-7 to be held in the spring season.

Bantam Girls D1 Funding - Motion made by Carolyn, seconded by Scott. Carried

NMBA to cover the $500 registration fee for Eastern Canadian Provincials for this team. Cheque issued to be payable to Basketball Nova Scotia.

Treasurer's Report

Natasha reported no major changed since May meeting. We will consolidate the 2 existing NMBA accounts into 1. Team managers and money management were discussed.

President's Report

Brad spoke briefly about next year's potential age division streamlining. Next meeting will be Tuesday, July 15.

Motion to adjourn at 8:40 made by John. Carried.
